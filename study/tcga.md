# Introduction

OS (Overall Survival): The patient is either dead or alive
- Usually not the best indicator because the patient might die of non-cancer reasons and it doesn't say anything about the aggressivenes, tumor biology, or responsiveness to therapy

DSS (Disease-specific survival)

DFI (Disease-free interval)

PFI (Progression-free interval)


DFI and PFI may be better because the patients develop disease recurrence or progression before dying of their disease
